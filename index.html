About:

CryptoGetExpo is a premier blockchain conference network that brings together influential figures and industry experts to reshape the future of finance. The upcoming edition of CryptoGetExpo is scheduled to take place in Dubai on July 15, 2024, featuring the participation of the world's leading crypto companies and blockchain entrepreneurs. As Dubai emerges as a global crypto hub, CryptoGetExpo serves as the gathering point for the worldwide community interested in blockchain and cryptocurrency, offering engaging discussions on cutting-edge financial technologies. Attendees can expect extensive networking opportunities and participation from over 30 countries, making it a truly global event.
